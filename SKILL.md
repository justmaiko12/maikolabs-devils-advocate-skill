# MaikoLabs Devils Advocate

Use this skill to challenge an idea before time is spent building it.

## Pressure Test

1. Restate the idea without hype.
2. Identify the intended audience and outcome.
3. List the strongest reasons it could work.
4. List the strongest reasons it could fail.
5. Flag unclear assumptions.
6. Identify what is over-scoped or unnecessary.
7. Recommend the sharper next move.

## Rules

- Be direct, not performative.
- Challenge weak thinking with concrete reasoning.
- Do not argue just to argue.
- If the idea is good, make it sharper.
- If the idea is weak, offer a better path.
